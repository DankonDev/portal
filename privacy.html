Privacy Policy

At Dankon Games, your privacy is our priority. We are committed to protecting and respecting your personal data in accordance with the European Union's General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

1. What data do we collect?

We collect the following personal data from you through our games and contact forms:

  • First name
  • Email address
  • In-game username
  • Device identifier

2. How do we collect your data?

Data is collected directly when you fill out contact forms on our website or when you use our games and applications.

3. For what purposes do we use your data?

We use your data to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and provide customer support
  • Analyze user behavior to improve our services (using Google Analytics)
  • Conduct marketing activities related to our mobile games (using device identifiers)

4. Third-party services

We use Google Analytics for website and game usage analytics, and Unity Ads for in-game advertisements. These services process data on our behalf under strict privacy agreements.

5. Cookies

Our website and games do not use cookies.

6. Your rights

You have the right to request access to, correction, or full deletion of your personal data. To do so, please use our contact form. We will respond promptly to your requests.

7. Data security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures, including encryption and restricted access, to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

8. Data sharing

We do not share your personal data with third parties, except when necessary to respond to your contact form requests.

9. Contact

For any questions or concerns regarding your personal data or this privacy policy, please reach out via our contact form.

Dankon Games operates as a sole proprietor based in Poland, European Union.
